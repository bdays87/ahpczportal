## Introduction

### About AHPCZ

The Allied Health Practitioners Council of Zimbabwe (AHPCZ) is the regulatory authority for health practitioners in Zimbabwe. The AHPCZ Portal is a comprehensive web-based system designed to manage practitioner registrations, renewals, continuing professional development (CDP), assessments, and regulatory compliance.

### Purpose of This Guide

This user guide provides step-by-step instructions for using the AHPCZ Portal System. It covers all major features and workflows for practitioners, students, and administrators.

### System Requirements

- **Web Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
- **Internet Connection**: Stable internet connection required
- **JavaScript**: Must be enabled
- **Screen Resolution**: Minimum 1024x768 pixels recommended

## System Overview

### Main Features

The AHPCZ Portal System includes the following key modules:

1. **User Management**: Registration, authentication, and profile management

7. **Certificate Verification**: Public certificate verification system

- **Real-time Updates**: Instant status updates on applications
- **Secure Payments**: Integrated payment gateway
- **Professional Development**: Track and manage CDP activities
- **Compliance Tracking**: Monitor registration status and requirements

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Portal

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to the AHPCZ Portal URL (provided by your administrator)
3. The welcome page will display public information and login options

### User Registration

#### For Practitioners

1. Click on **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"** on the welcome page
2. Select **"Practitioner"** as your account type
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Name**: Your first name
   - **Surname**: Your last name
   - **Email**: Valid email address (will be used for login)
   - **Phone**: Contact phone number
   - **Password**: Minimum 8 characters
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password
4. Click **"Submit"**
5. You will receive a confirmation message
6. Proceed to the login page

#### For Students

1. Follow the same registration process as practitioners
2. Select **"Student"** as your account type
3. Complete the registration form
4. Submit and proceed to login

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your **Email** address
3. Enter your **Password**
4. Click **"Login"**
5. You will be redirected to your dashboard based on your account type

### Password Recovery

If you forget your password:

1. Click **"Forget Password"** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click **"Submit"**
4. Check your email for password reset instructions
5. Follow the link in the email to reset your password

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you will see your personalized dashboard:

- **Practitioner Dashboard**: Shows applications, renewals, CDP points, invoices, and notifications
- **Student Dashboard**: Displays registration status, applications, and academic information
- **Administrator Dashboard**: Provides system statistics, pending approvals, and management tools

## Practitioner Workflows

### Profile Management

#### Updating Personal Information

1. Navigate to **Profile Settings** from the dashboard
2. Update the following sections:
   - **Personal Details**: Name, surname, date of birth, gender, nationality
   - **Contact Details**: Email, phone, address
   - **Employment Details**: Employment status, location, employer information
   - **Bank Details**: Banking information for payments
3. Click **"Save"** after each section

#### Uploading Documents

1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Navigate to the **Documents** section
3. Click **"Upload Document"**
4. Select document type (certificate, ID, qualification, etc.)
5. Choose file from your computer
6. Click **"Upload"**
7. Wait for upload confirmation

### New Application Process

#### Step 1: Application Invoicing

1. Navigate to **New Applications** → **Practitioners**
2. Select **"Application Invoicing"**
3. System will generate an invoice for application fees
4. Review invoice details:
   - Invoice number
   - Amount
   - Currency
   - Description
5. Proceed to payment (see Payment Processing section)

#### Step 2: Document Upload

1. After payment confirmation, go to **Document Upload**
2. Upload required documents:
   - Academic qualifications
   - Professional certificates
   - Identity documents
   - Proof of payment
   - Other supporting documents
3. Ensure all documents are clear and legible
4. Click **"Submit Documents"**

#### Step 3: Qualifications Capture

1. Navigate to **Qualifications Capture**
2. Add your qualifications:
   - Qualification name
   - Institution
   - Year obtained
   - Qualification level
   - Category
3. Upload supporting certificates
4. Click **"Save Qualification"**
5. Repeat for all qualifications

#### Step 4: Assessment Invoicing

1. If assessment is required, navigate to **Assessment Invoicing**
2. Review assessment fees
3. Complete payment
4. Wait for assessment scheduling

#### Step 5: Registration Invoicing

1. After assessment approval, go to **Registration Invoicing**
2. Review registration fees
3. Complete payment
4. Application will be submitted for approval

### Application Status Tracking

1. Go to **My Applications** from dashboard
2. View application status:
   - **PENDING**: Awaiting review
   - **APPROVED**: Application approved
   - **REJECTED**: Application rejected (with reasons)
   - **IN_PROGRESS**: Under review
3. Click on an application to view details
4. Check for any required actions or additional documents

### Renewal Process

#### Checking Renewal Eligibility

1. Navigate to **My Professions**
2. Select the profession you want to renew
3. Check:
   - Current registration status
   - CDP points accumulated
   - Minimum CDP requirements
   - Expiry date

#### Renewal Requirements

Before renewing, ensure you have:
- Sufficient CDP points (check minimum requirements)
- Valid registration
- All documents up to date
- Payment method ready

#### Initiating Renewal

1. Go to **Renew Application**
2. Select profession to renew
3. Choose renewal type:
   - **Renew**: Obtain valid practising certificate
   - **Maintain**: Be included in maintenance register
4. Select application year
5. Review renewal fees (including penalties if applicable)
6. Complete payment
7. Upload any required renewal documents
8. Submit renewal application

#### Renewal Status

Monitor renewal status:
- **PENDING**: Awaiting approval
- **APPROVED**: Renewal approved, certificate issued
- **REJECTED**: Renewal rejected (check reasons)

### Continuing Professional Development (CDP)

#### Viewing CDP Points

1. Navigate to **My Professions**
2. Select a profession
3. View **CDP Points** section:
   - Total points accumulated
   - Points required for renewal
   - Points by year
   - Activity breakdown

#### Submitting CDP Activities

1. Go to **My CDP** or **CDP Management**
2. Click **"Add CDP Activity"**
3. Fill in activity details:
   - **Title**: Activity name
   - **Type**: Workshop, conference, course, etc.
   - **Duration**: Hours or days
   - **Year**: Activity year
   - **Description**: Detailed description
   - **Points**: Expected points (if known)
4. Upload supporting documents:
   - Certificates of attendance
   - Program schedules
   - Other evidence
5. Click **"Submit"**
6. Status will show as **"PENDING"** until admin approval

#### CDP Approval Process

1. Admin reviews your CDP submission
2. Points are assigned based on activity value
3. You receive notification of approval/rejection
4. Approved points are added to your total
5. View updated points in **My Professions**

### Professional Development Activities

#### Browsing Activities

1. Navigate to **My Activities**
2. View available activities:
   - **My Enrollments**: Activities you're enrolled in
   - **Available Activities**: Activities for your profession
3. Filter by:
   - Profession
   - Activity type
   - Status

#### Enrolling in Activities

1. Browse **Available Activities**
2. Click on an activity to view details:
   - Description
   - Type (video, article, attachment)
   - Points available
   - Duration
3. Click **"Enroll Now"**
4. Confirmation message will appear

#### Completing Activities

1. Go to **My Enrollments**
2. Select an enrolled activity
3. Access activity content:
   - **Video**: Watch embedded video
   - **Article**: Read article content
   - **Attachment**: Download and review files
4. After reviewing content, click **"Take Quiz"** (if available)

#### Taking Quizzes

1. Click **"Take Quiz"** on enrolled activity
2. Review quiz instructions:
   - Number of questions
   - Time limit (if applicable)
   - Pass percentage
   - Points available
3. Click **"Start Quiz"**
4. Answer questions:
   - **Single Choice**: Select one answer
   - **Multiple Choice**: Select all correct answers
   - **True/False**: Select true or false
5. Use navigation sidebar to move between questions
6. Monitor progress bar
7. Click **"Submit Quiz"** when complete
8. View results:
   - Score percentage
   - Points earned
   - Pass/fail status
   - Correct answers review

#### Quiz Retakes

- Check maximum attempts allowed
- If failed, you can retake (within attempt limit)
- Best score counts toward final grade
- Points are awarded based on performance

## Payment Processing

### Payment Methods

The system supports multiple payment methods:

1. **Online Payments**:
   - Credit/Debit cards
   - Mobile money
   - Bank transfers via payment gateway

2. **Manual Payments**:
   - Bank deposits
   - Cash payments (at office)
   - Bank transfers

### Online Payment Process

1. Select invoice to pay
2. Click **"Pay Now"**
3. Choose payment method
4. Enter payment details:
   - Card number (for cards)
   - Mobile money number
   - Bank account details
5. Confirm payment amount
6. Complete payment
7. Receive payment confirmation
8. Invoice automatically updates to **"PAID"**

### Manual Payment Process

1. Select invoice
2. Note invoice details:
   - Invoice number
   - Amount
   - Reference number
3. Make payment via approved method
4. Upload proof of payment:
   - Bank deposit slip
   - Transfer confirmation
   - Receipt
5. Enter payment reference
6. Submit proof
7. Wait for admin verification (24-48 hours)
8. Receive confirmation when verified

### Payment Verification

For manual payments:
1. Admin reviews proof of payment
2. Verifies payment details:
   - Amount matches invoice
   - Reference number correct
   - Payment method valid
3. Matches payment to invoice
4. Updates invoice status
5. Sends confirmation to customer

### Payment Issues

If payment issues occur:

1. **Payment Not Reflected**:
   - Wait 24-48 hours for processing
   - Check payment confirmation email
   - Contact support with payment reference

2. **Payment Rejected**:
   - Check payment details
   - Verify sufficient funds
   - Try alternative payment method
   - Contact support

3. **Duplicate Payment**:
   - Contact support immediately
   - Provide payment references
   - Request refund if applicable

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Login Problems

**Issue**: Cannot log in
- **Solution**: 
  - Verify email and password
  - Check caps lock
  - Use password reset if needed
  - Clear browser cache
  - Try different browser

**Issue**: Account locked
- **Solution**: 
  - Wait 15 minutes
